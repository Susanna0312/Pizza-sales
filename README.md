# Pizza-sales
This project analyzes pizza sales data to uncover key business insights such as total revenue, best-selling pizzas, peak order times. Using Excel/SQL the dataset is cleaned, transformed, and visualized to help 
understand sales performance and support data-driven decision-making

# Problem statement
-Analyze overall pizza sales performance

-Identify top-selling and least-selling pizzas

-Understand customer demand by time, size, and category

-Track revenue trends and order volume

-Provide actionable insights for business growth

# Dataset Overview
-Order ID and order date

-Pizza name, category, and size

-Quantity ordered

-Price and total sales value

# Key Insights
-Total revenue and total number of orders

-Most popular pizza categories and sizes

-Daily, monthly, and hourly sales trends

-High-performing and low-performing products

# Tools and Technologies
-Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts)

-SQL (Data querying and analysis)

# key insights from pizza sales
**Overall Business Performance**
-The business generated strong total revenue with a healthy number of orders, indicating consistent customer demand.

-The average order value shows that customers typically purchase more than one item per order.

-On average, customers order more than 2 pizzas per transaction, which is a positive sign for upselling and combo strategies.

# Sales Trends
Order trends show clear peak periods, indicating specific days or times when customer demand is highest.

**These peak periods are ideal for:**

-Running promotions

-Increasing staffing

-Ensuring sufficient inventory

# Category & Size Preferences

-Certain pizza categories contribute a higher percentage of total sales, making them the core revenue drivers.

-Popular pizza sizes dominate sales, suggesting strong customer preference and helping guide pricing and inventory decisions.

# Top and Bottom Performers
**Top-selling pizzas consistently drive revenue and should be:**

-Promoted more aggressively

-Featured in deals and combos

**Bottom-selling pizzas indicate opportunities to:**

-Improve recipes

-Reprice

-Bundle with popular items  Or remove from the menu to reduce costs
# Actionable Business Recommendations
-Focus marketing efforts on top-performing pizzas and peak sales periods

-Optimize inventory based on high-demand categories and sizes

-Use data insights to design combo offers to increase average order value

-Review low-performing products to improve profitability
